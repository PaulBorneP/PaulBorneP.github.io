He/Him

I am a doctoral student in Imagine lab at ENPC under the supervision Loic Landrieu, Odran Sourdeval, Kara D. Lamb and Mikolaj Czerkawski.

At the intersection of deep learning, remote sensing and Earth sciences, my PhD focuses on developing machine learning methods to derive 3D cloud properties from multimodal sources.

I Before my PhD, I graduated from the MVA master (Mathematiques, Vision et Apprentissage) at ENS Paris Saclay and got an engineering degree from CentraleSupélec with a specialization in data science.
